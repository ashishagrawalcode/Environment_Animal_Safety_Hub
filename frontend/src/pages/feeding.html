Safe Feeding Guidelines

What to Feed

Natural foods like grains, fruits, vegetables. Species-specific commercial food when available.

What to Avoid

No processed human food, chocolate, salty snacks, or bread (causes malnutrition).

Water Matters

Always provide clean, fresh water. Change daily to prevent contamination.

Smart Feeding Tips

Consistent Timing

Animals thrive on routine. Feed at same times daily to establish trust.

Clean Bowls

Wash feeding containers daily to prevent bacterial growth and disease.

Safe Locations

Choose quiet, sheltered spots away from traffic and predators.

No Hand Feeding

Maintain distance. Wild animals should not associate humans with food.
